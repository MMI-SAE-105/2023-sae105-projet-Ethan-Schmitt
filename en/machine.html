War Machine Volume 3: A Whirlwind of Conflicts

Ethan Schmitt

08/01/2024

The story takes place in 1944, during World War II. Five American soldiers are selected to form the crew of a Sherman tank, nicknamed "Dream Team". They are trained by an instructor who teaches them the basics of armored combat and warns them of the dangers of the front line. The Sherman tank is fast and maneuverable, but it is inferior to German tanks like the Tiger or the Panther.

On D-Day, June 6, 1944, the Dream Team lands on Omaha Beach in Normandy. They must join the US 3rd Armored Division Spearhead, which is tasked with breaking through enemy lines and advancing towards Paris. But the path is filled with obstacles: mines, traps, ambushes, snipers, planes... The Dream Team must show courage, solidarity, and ingenuity to survive and accomplish their mission. As they face the battles, the five soldiers will forge friendships, but also experience fear, pain, and death. They will encounter historical figures such as General Patton, Colonel Abrams, or the resistance fighter Jean Moulin. They will also discover the reality of war, with its horrors, atrocities, and moral dilemmas.

The comic book War Machines Volume 3 - Dream Team is a historical and realistic narrative that pays tribute to the soldiers who fought for freedom. It combines action, suspense, humor, and emotion. It is accompanied by dynamic and precise artwork that faithfully recreates the settings, weapons, and vehicles of the time.
