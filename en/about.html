About

Hello, my name is Ethan Schmitt, I'm 19 years old and I'm a first-year student in MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions). I am passionate about cinema and World War II. I created this website to share my passion for the movie 'Fury'. This film deeply impacted me with its realistic approach to the history of the men who fought aboard tanks. Furthermore, the realism depicted in the film illustrates the horror that World War II could represent. My approach to the project is simple: I want to create a space where fans of the film can learn more about certain aspects of the film, discover similar titles, as well as a comic book, and learn more about the famous M4A3E8 Sherman tank featured in the film. I hope this site will help you deepen your understanding and appreciation of "Fury". I invite you to explore the site, learn more about the film, and share your own passion for "Fury". Feel free to contact me if you have any questions or suggestions.
