Other Films

Apocalypse Now (1979) directed by Francis Ford Coppola

A destabilizing journey into the heart of the Vietnam War, where a captain is tasked with finding and eliminating a rogue colonel.

Full Metal Jacket (1987), directed by Stanley Kubrick

A ruthless look at the process of dehumanization of soldiers during the Vietnam War, through the eyes of a young recruit.

Saving Private Ryan (1998) directed by Steven Spielberg

A perilous mission to save a paratrooper whose brothers were killed in combat during World War II.

The Thin Red Line (1998) directed by Terrence Malick

A poetic and visceral meditation on the nature of war, centered around the Battle of Guadalcanal during World War II.

The Deer Hunter (1978) directed by Michael Cimino

A heartbreaking story about the impact of the Vietnam War on the lives of three friends from a small industrial town.

1917 (2019) directed by Sam Mendes

A gripping and immersive tale that follows two young British soldiers tasked with delivering a crucial message to prevent a disastrous attack during World War I.
